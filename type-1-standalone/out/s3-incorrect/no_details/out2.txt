   *Add a bucket policy that rejects any non‑HTTPS request.*

3. **Block public access for all buckets (CIS S3.8)**  
   *Apply the “Block all public access” setting to every bucket, including *musa‑webapp*.*

4. **Enable MFA Delete (CIS S3.20)**  
   *Turn on MFA Delete for each bucket that holds critical or historical data.*

5. **Review bucket policies**  
   *Ensure that only trusted IAM roles or accounts have write permissions.*
